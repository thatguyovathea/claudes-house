Security Operations 3 / 10
Think as a manager, not a technician. Which answer best protects the organization's mission — not just the technical system?
An organization experiences a ransomware attack that encrypts critical servers. The incident response team has identified the attack vector. As CISO, what should be your FIRST priority?
A
Restore all systems from the most recent backup immediately
B
Pay the ransom to minimize business disruption
C
Contain the spread and preserve forensic evidence before recovery
D
Notify law enforcement and wait for their guidance
Why C is correct

Before any recovery action, containment prevents the ransomware from spreading to additional systems. Forensic preservation ensures you can identify the root cause and prevent recurrence — and may be legally required.

Option A is premature — restoring before containment risks re-infection. Option B violates most organizational and legal policies. Option D delays critical containment actions.

CISSP mindset: The "best answer" prioritizes preservation of the organization's ability to function long-term, not just the fastest short-term fix.
